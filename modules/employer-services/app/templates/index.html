About Employer Verification

When a former employee files for unemployment benefits, we need you to verify their employment details. This helps ensure the accuracy of benefit calculations and prevents improper payments.

You will receive notifications when new verification requests are available. Please respond to these requests promptly to avoid delays in processing.
